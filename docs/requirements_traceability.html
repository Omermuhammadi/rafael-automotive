Scope decisions on record

The build draws two kinds of deliberate line: four requirements sit fully outside the accepted offer, and two more are bounded to their doable slice — grouped below as five decisions (Pillar B covers two rows). None are omissions; each was agreed up front in SCOPE.md to protect a 7-day delivery of the two pillars that were paid for. Any can be pulled into scope — that's a scope-and-timeline call for you to make.

  • Pillar B / Tab 2Live OBD-II telematics. Needs a physical ELM327/STN adapter + vehicle to validate honestly; wasn't part of the two-pillar offer.
  • OCPP 2.0.1MVP is 1.6J only. 2.0.1 is additive and can layer on later without rework.
  • Map auto-discoveryReverse-engineering maps from unknown binaries has no finish line. Fixed-offset scanning — the spec's own wording — is included.
  • Universal checksum engineEvery ECU family rolls its own scheme. CRC16/32 + block-sum ship; one ECU-specific scheme if the target is cheap.
  • On-vehicle I/O validationThe real cable path is validated by you, on your vehicles — the seam is built and mock-proven on our side.
